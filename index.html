    🗄️ Archive or Delete Profile

    Choose what to do with 's profile.

    🗄️ Archive (Recommended)
    Sets the employee's status to Separated. The profile and all HR history are kept on file for records. This can be undone by editing the profile.
    🗑️ Permanently Delete
    Removes the profile record entirely. This cannot be undone. Associated call-out logs, time off requests, and warning history are not deleted.
